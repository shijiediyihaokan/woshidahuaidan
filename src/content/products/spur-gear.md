## Product Features

Spur gears are the most common type of gear used in mechanical power transmission. Our spur gears are CNC-machined to exact specifications, delivering reliable performance across a wide range of industrial applications.

The parallel shaft design provides efficient torque transfer with minimal axial load, making spur gears ideal for conveyors, pumping systems, and general machinery.

## Technical Parameters

Our manufacturing capabilities cover modules from 0.5 to 20 with outer diameters up to 2000mm. We work with carbon steels, alloy steels, and stainless steels, each selected for specific application requirements.

Heat treatment options including carburizing, quenching, and nitriding enhance surface hardness while maintaining core toughness for extended service life.

## Applications

Spur gears are essential components in:
- Industrial conveyor systems and material handling equipment
- Pump drives and compressor transmissions
- Agricultural machinery and harvesting equipment
- Textile machinery and printing presses
- Automotive transmissions and differential systems

## FAQ

Q: What materials are best for spur gears?
A: 20CrMnTi and 40Cr are ideal for high-load applications requiring wear resistance. 45# steel works well for medium-duty use. Stainless steel 304/316 is recommended for food processing or corrosive environments.

Q: Can you manufacture custom tooth profiles?
A: Yes. We produce standard involute profiles as well as custom cycloidal profiles. Send us your drawings for a quotation.

Q: What surface treatments are available?
A: We offer black oxide for corrosion resistance, zinc plating for moderate protection, and phosphating for improved lubricity during break-in periods.
