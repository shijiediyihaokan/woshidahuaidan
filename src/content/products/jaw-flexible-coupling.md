Jaw couplings are the workhorse of industrial power transmission — simple, reliable, and cost-effective. The three-piece design consists of two metal hubs and a replaceable elastomeric spider.

## Spider Options

- **NBR (Nitrile Rubber)** — Standard material, good damping, -40°C to +80°C
- **Polyurethane (PU)** — Higher torque capacity, better chemical resistance
- **Hytrel (TPE)** — High temperature to +120°C, excellent fatigue life

## Misalignment Tolerance

| Type | NBR Spider | PU/Hytrel Spider |
|------|-----------|------------------|
| Angular | 1.0° | 0.5° |
| Parallel | 0.38 mm | 0.25 mm |
| Axial | ±1.0 mm | ±0.5 mm |

This makes jaw couplings ideal for standard motor-pump and motor-gearbox connections where perfect alignment is impractical.
