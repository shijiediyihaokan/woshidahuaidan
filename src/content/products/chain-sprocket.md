Guangjin sprockets are manufactured to the highest standards using CNC machining centers with hobbing, shaping, or milling processes depending on the size and quantity.

## Tooth Profile

All sprockets conform to ISO 606 and ANSI B29.1 standards for chain engagement geometry. The tooth profile is optimized for:
- Smooth roller engagement and disengagement
- Even load distribution across multiple teeth
- Reduced noise and vibration at high speeds
- Maximum chain/sprocket service life

## Custom Sprockets

We manufacture custom sprockets to your drawings or samples. Specify your bore diameter, keyway dimensions, set screw positions, and any special hub configuration.
