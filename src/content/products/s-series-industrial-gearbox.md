The S series industrial gearbox combines a helical input stage with a worm output stage, achieving high reduction ratios while maintaining a compact footprint. This configuration provides the cost advantages of worm gearing with improved efficiency from the helical primary stage.

## Heavy-Duty Design

The HT250 cast iron housing features extensive ribbing for heat dissipation and structural stiffness. All shafts run on high-quality tapered roller bearings rated for combined radial and axial loads, making the S series suitable for demanding applications such as conveyor drives in mining and quarrying.

## Cooling Options

For continuous-duty applications, the S series can be equipped with:
- External cooling fan with aluminum shroud
- Oil cooler with circulation pump
- Forced air cooling package

These options extend the thermal power rating by 30-60% compared to natural cooling.
