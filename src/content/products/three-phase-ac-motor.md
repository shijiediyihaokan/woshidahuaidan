## Product Features

Our three-phase AC induction motors deliver reliable, efficient power for industrial applications worldwide. Built to IEC standards with premium IE3/IE4 efficiency ratings, these motors reduce energy costs while providing consistent performance.

The robust construction includes high-grade silicon steel laminations, Class F insulation, and precision-balanced rotors for smooth, vibration-free operation. Every motor undergoes a comprehensive test program before shipment.

## Technical Specifications

Available from 0.18kW to 315kW across IEC frame sizes 56-355. Standard voltages include 220/380V, 380/660V, and 400/690V at 50Hz or 60Hz. Speed options: 2-pole (3000 rpm), 4-pole (1500 rpm), 6-pole (1000 rpm), and 8-pole (750 rpm).

Aluminum frames are standard for sizes up to 132, with cast iron frames for larger sizes. All motors offer IP55 protection for use in dusty or wet environments.

## Applications

Three-phase induction motors power:
- Water pumps and irrigation systems
- Industrial fans and blowers
- Air compressors and vacuum pumps
- Conveyor belts and material handling
- Machine tools and woodworking equipment
- HVAC systems and cooling towers

## FAQ

Q: What is the difference between IE2, IE3, and IE4?
A: IE2 is standard efficiency, IE3 is premium efficiency (mandatory in EU since 2021), and IE4 is super premium. IE4 motors offer 10-15% lower energy consumption compared to IE2.

Q: Can the motor run on 60Hz power?
A: Yes. Motors rated for 50Hz can generally run on 60Hz with approximately 20% higher speed and power. Please specify your power supply when ordering for optimal winding configuration.
