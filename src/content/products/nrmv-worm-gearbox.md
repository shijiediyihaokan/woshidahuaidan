The NMRV series worm gearbox is our flagship product, engineered for reliable performance in industrial and commercial applications. Combining a robust worm gear mechanism with a lightweight aluminum housing, this gearbox delivers exceptional torque in a compact footprint.

## Design Features

The NMRV worm gearbox features a precision-ground worm shaft made from case-hardened alloy steel, meshing with a high-quality bronze worm wheel. This combination ensures smooth, quiet operation and excellent wear resistance.

The die-cast aluminum housing provides superior heat dissipation while keeping the unit lightweight — a critical advantage in mobile equipment and space-constrained installations.

## Applications

NMRV worm gearboxes are widely used across industries:

- **Conveyor Systems** — Speed reduction and torque multiplication for belt, roller, and chain conveyors
- **Packaging Machinery** — Precise indexing and positioning in filling, capping, and labeling machines
- **Food Processing** — Stainless steel output shaft options for washdown environments
- **Material Handling** — Lifts, hoists, and palletizers
- **Automation** — Servo-driven positioning systems requiring backlash control

## Mounting Flexibility

One of the NMRV's key advantages is its mounting versatility. Available in foot-mounted, flange-mounted, and shaft-mounted configurations, it adapts to virtually any installation requirement without complex adapter plates.

## Quality Standards

Every NMRV gearbox undergoes 100% testing before shipment, including noise level checks, temperature rise monitoring, and load testing. We use high-grade NSK/SKF bearings and premium lubricants to ensure long service intervals.
