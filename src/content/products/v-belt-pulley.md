The taper bush pulley system represents the industry standard for industrial V-belt drives. The split taper bush provides a mechanical interference fit on the shaft without the need for keyway damage or shaft galling.

## Taper Bush Advantages

- **Quick Installation** — Slide on, tighten screws, done
- **Self-Centering** — Automatic concentricity without shimming
- **Easy Removal** — Loosen screws, use jacking holes to release
- **Interchangeable** — Change bore size by swapping bushes only

## Balancing

All pulleys are dynamically balanced to ISO 1940 Grade G6.3 as standard. Grade G2.5 balancing is available for high-speed applications above 3,000 rpm.
