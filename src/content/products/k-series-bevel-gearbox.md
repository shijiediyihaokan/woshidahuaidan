The K series helical bevel gearbox combines the efficiency of helical gearing with the directional flexibility of bevel gears, delivering right-angle power transmission without the efficiency losses typical of worm gearboxes.

## Right-Angle Design

The bevel gear stage provides a precise 90° turn in the power flow, enabling motor and driven shaft to be mounted perpendicular to each other. This saves significant installation space compared to inline arrangements.

## Universal Housing

The K series is famous for its universal housing design — a single gearbox can be mounted in any of six orientations (M1 through M6) without modification. Mounting feet, flanges, and shaft positions are fully interchangeable, dramatically reducing inventory requirements.

## Robust Construction

The HT250 cast iron housing provides excellent vibration damping and structural rigidity. Combined with oversized bearings, this makes the K series ideal for demanding applications such as:

- Bucket elevators and belt conveyors handling shock loads
- Crane trolley and bridge drives
- Heavy-duty mixers and agitators
- Industrial washing systems with frequent reversing
