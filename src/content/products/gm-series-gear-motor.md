Guangjin gear motors are fully integrated drive solutions — motor and gearbox matched, assembled, and tested as a single unit. This eliminates the complexity of sourcing and coupling separate components.

## Integrated Design Benefits

- **Simplified Procurement** — One part number instead of three (motor, gearbox, coupling)
- **Guaranteed Compatibility** — Motor and gearbox engineered together
- **Reduced Assembly Time** — Bolt-on installation, pre-aligned
- **Compact Footprint** — Shorter overall length than coupled solutions
- **Lower Maintenance** — Fewer wearing parts, single lubrication point

## Motor Options

We offer gear motors with:

- **Standard AC Motors** — IE3 premium efficiency, 2/4/6 pole
- **Brake Motors** — DC or AC electromagnetic brake, fail-safe
- **Inverter-Duty Motors** — For VFD speed control, Class F insulation with Class B rise
- **Single-Phase Motors** — For locations without three-phase power

## Quality Testing

Each gear motor undergoes a 40-point inspection including no-load current, vibration analysis, temperature rise, and load testing before shipment.
